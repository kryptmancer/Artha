How to Use
  1. Click Start Speaking and allow microphone access if prompted
  2. Speak clearly in Nepali for at least 3 seconds
  3. Click Stop when you're done speaking
  4. Wait for processing to complete
  5. View your transcript and translation
Tip: For better results, speak in a quiet environment and pronounce words clearly.
Troubleshooting Tips:
  • If you're getting "No speech detected", try speaking louder or getting closer to your microphone
  • For better recognition, use simple, common Nepali phrases
  • Avoid background noise when recording
  • If stuck in "Processing..." for too long, refresh the page and try again
  • Check that your microphone is working in other applications
